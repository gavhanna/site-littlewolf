BORDALO II

Forgotten Project / Rome


Bordalo II creates ‘Uma Cabra’ at St. Peter’s Train station minutes walk from the Vatican for the third artistic intervention by Forgotten Project. This aims to highlight some central buildings of the city of Rome that, due to several factors, are going to be somehow "forgotten". Thanks to this project, five Portuguese artists, very popular in recent years in Europe and all over the world, are invited for the first time in Rome to realize urban artworks, everyone with their own personal technique.​


'Well I’m not a teacher, or a God or whatever, but I want to contribute with something true and genuine, to give an option for those who want to see what’s hidden.' Bordalo II


Directed by Trev Whelan + Leonardo Meuti
