COREY DIVINE

“Together, utilizing Sacred Geometry Art, we can raise the vibration of the planet and uplift humanity.” ~ Corey Divine


LA based Corey Divine is a tattoo artist who focuses on sacred geometry. Mostly in blackwork, but sometimes using colors, he creates intricate and impressive pieces of sacred geometric patterns. For my first tattoo I met Corey in London and together with Rua Meegan captured some smooth moments of him at work.
